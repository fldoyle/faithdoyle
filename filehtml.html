About the CEO

AI Expert | Software Engineer
Keynote Speaker & EMCEE (Master of Ceremony)

I am the founder of EMEA Venture Builder and NEEMA AI, passionate about building fair, transparent, and inclusive AI. My background spans software engineering, data science, economics, and full-stack web development.

At EMEA Venture Builder, I help organizations automate tasks, use predictive analytics, and implement custom LLM-powered solutions. At NEEMA AI, I provide AI ethics training in fairness, privacy, governance, and accountability.

I hold two MSc degrees: one in Business & Research (University of Barcelona), another in Economy & Management (University of Annecy), and diplomas in Data Science and Full-Stack Web Development.

As a keynote speaker and MC, I engage audiences on responsible AI and its social impact. In 2022, I was honored as an Ambassador of Barcelona, and in 2024 named Tech Leader of the Year by Women Who Change the World.
